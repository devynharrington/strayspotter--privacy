StraySpotter

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information StraySpotter collects, how that information is used, and what choices users have when using the app.

Effective date: April 5, 2026

Overview

StraySpotter helps users view, post, and discuss animal sightings. The app uses account, location, image, and user-generated content data to support core functionality.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in using a supported login method.
  • User content you provide, including sightings, comments, replies, uploaded images, and profile selections made in the app.
  • Location information used when creating or viewing sightings.
  • Technical information required to operate Firebase-backed authentication, database, storage, and notification features.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To let you post and view sightings, comments, and replies.
  • To store uploaded sighting images and display your posting identity in the app.
  • To support in-app notifications, moderation, safety, abuse prevention, and core app operations.

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time in the app by going to Profile > Delete Account.

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile information, sightings, comments, replies, notifications, and uploaded sighting images from StraySpotter.

Limited moderation-related records may be retained only when necessary for safety, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

If you sign in again after deleting your account, a new account may be created and previously deleted StraySpotter data will not be restored.

Data Sharing

StraySpotter does not sell your personal information. The app relies on service providers such as Firebase, Google Sign-In, and Sign in with Apple to operate authentication, storage, database, and notification features.

Data Retention

Account and app data are kept only as long as needed to operate StraySpotter, unless you delete your account or a different retention period is required for legal or safety reasons.

Children

StraySpotter is not designed specifically for children.
